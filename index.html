About Us

Control Systems Integrated specializes in Electrical, Instrumentation and Hazardous Area Compliance across various sectors.

Our Services

✅ Test and Tag

We perform thorough Testing and Tagging of all electrical appliances to ensure they are safe and compliant with AS/NZS 3760 Standards.

✅ Test and Tag Compliance

We help your business stay fully compliant with Electrical Safety Regulations. Our service includes:

  • Routine testing schedules
  • Compliance reporting
  • Emergency Exit Light Testing
  • RCD Testing
  • Recordkeeping to support safe operations and audit readiness

✅ Industrial Instrumentation

We provide expert installation and calibration of industrial Instrumentation systems. Our services include:

  • Pressure
  • Temperature
  • Flow
  • Control Valves

Our services ensure accurate measurements and reliable performance across your facility.

✅ Hazardous Area Inspections

Our certified Inspectors assess and verify electrical installations in Hazardous Areas where flammable gases, vapors, or dusts may be present. All inspections are carried out in accordance with AS/NZS 60079 Standards for Explosive Atmospheres.

✅ Hazardous Area Document Writing

We provide professionally written and compliant documentation for hazardous areas, including:

  • Verification Dossiers
  • Inspection Schedules
  • Hazardous Area Standards (Design, Electrical Equipment, Maintenance, Overhaul, Portable, Testing, Verification, Competency, Governance and Classification in Hazardous Areas)

This documentation is essential for both legal compliance and safe operations.
