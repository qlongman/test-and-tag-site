About Us

Control Systems Integrated is a trusted provider of professional Electrical Compliance, Test and Tag services, and Hazardous Area Services across WA. We ensure safety and regulatory compliance in every industry we serve.

Our Services

✅ Test and Tag (AS/NZS 3760 - AS/NZS 3000)

We provide comprehensive Test and Tag and Electrical services to meet the AS/NZS 3760 and AS/NZS 3000 standards. Our testing ensures all portable appliances are safe and compliant.

  • Routine testing and tagging
  • Detailed compliance reports
  • Emergency light and RCD testing
  • On-site service scheduling

✅ Hazardous Area Inspections (AS/NZS 60079)

We specialise in Hazardous Areas and electrical compliance. Our EEHA-qualified inspectors perform:

  • Visual and detailed inspections
  • Equipment verification
  • Compliance with AS/NZS 60079 series standards

✅ Industrial Instrumentation

Our team delivers expert installation, calibration, and maintenance of Instrumentation Systems including:

  • Pressure
  • Flow
  • Temperature
  • Level

✅ Hazardous Area Documentation

We develop accurate, audit-ready documentation for facilities operating in hazardous areas. This includes:

  • Verification Dossiers
  • EEHA Inspection Schedules
  • Equipment Registers and Compliance Checklists
  • Writing of Hazardous Area Standards specific to your site (Design, Maintenance, Overhaul, Portable, Testing, Verification, Competency, Governance and Calssification in Hazardous Areas)

Our documentation supports full compliance with AS/NZS 60079 and Australian regulatory requirements.
